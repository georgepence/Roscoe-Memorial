Richmond SPCA

Like 4,000 homeless animals each year, Roscoe was taken in by the Richmond SPCA , a no-kill humane society that saves pets from all over Virginia. The Richmond SPCA supports their principle that every life is precious by:

  • Operating a low cost full veterinary hospital for the pets of people with modest means
  • Offering a behavior helpline and resource library
  • Runs more than 30 pet training classes per week
  • Delivering free sterilization and rabies vaccinations to community cats under a Trap-Neuter-Return progam
