Richmond SPCA

Like 4,000 homeless animals each year, Roscoe was taken in by the Richmond SPCA, a no-kill humane society that saves pets from all over Virginia. The Richmond SPCA operates a low-cost full veterinary hospital for the pets of people with modest means, offers a behavior helpline and resource library, and runs more than 30 pet training classes per week.
